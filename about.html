About the Firm

Sanjay Seeds Corporations is a premium seed business established in 1998, focused on soyabean and other agricultural seed categories. The firm supplies organic, hybrid, research and certified seeds across Gujarat, Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh.

Mission

Growing prosperity through trusted seeds. The mission is to provide farmers and dealers with high-quality, reliable and performance-focused seeds backed by honest guidance and long-term relationships.

Vision

To expand across India while preserving agricultural heritage, combining traditional farming values with modern research, strong dealer networks and future export possibilities.

Values

Quality before quantity, farmer trust before transaction, transparent dealing, timely supply, and action more than words.

Business Highlights

Branch & Supply

Main location is linked through Google Maps. Maharashtra has both manager office and godown support. The company supplies Gujarat, Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh.
